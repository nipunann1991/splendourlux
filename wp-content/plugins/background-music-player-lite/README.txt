Background music player for your website.

== Description ==
Background music player allows you to play tracks and radio streams right on your WordPress website. 
This plugin has lots of useful options and can be fully customized.

Visit [demo website](http://wpdemo.circlewaves.com/background-music-player/ "demo website") and [watch video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R1AJFeS4xIs "watch video")

Buy [Full Version](http://codecanyon.net/item/background-music-player/7312865?ref=circlewaves "http://codecanyon.net/item/background-music-player/7312865")

[youtube https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R1AJFeS4xIs]


** Features: **

**Continuous playback in the popup**
*Continuous Playback popup feature allows website visitor to open player in the popup. You can customize popup window size, window background color and image and player colors.*

**Unlimited color schemes**
*Choose color for player background and buttons*

**4 built-in styles**
*Choose one of 4 built-in styles*

**Supports radio streams**
*Broadcast your favorite radio through your website*

**Custom playlists for pages and posts**
*Define specific tracks for each page or post*

**Auto-play and playlist repeat options**
*Automatically play music when page loads*

**Shuffle and tracks sort order option**
*Sort tracks as you wish*

**Cross-browser and mobile friendly**
*Compatible with all major browsers (even IE 7) and mobile devices (iPhone 4/5 , Windows Phone and Android)*

**Auto-include and shortcode options**
*Include player automatically or use shortcode*

== Installation ==

1. Use native WordPress Plugin Installer or Download and unzip plugin to "/wp-content/plugins/" directory
2. Activate plugin in "Plugins" Menu
3. Configure plugin. [Watch video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R1AJFeS4xIs "Watch video") and see [guidelines and documentation](http://docs.circlewaves.com/background-music-player/ "guidelines and documentation")
